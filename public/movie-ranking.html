The Best Movies According to Vaishnavi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" is a Bollywood rom-com about four friends who meet on a Himalayan trek. Bunny and Naina fall in love but Bunny's love for travel causes commitment issues. Bunny becomes a successful videographer, but still longs for Naina. As they reconnect, they navigate love and ambition. The film is a heartwarming coming-of-age story with catchy songs, picturesque locations and an ensemble cast.

Jab We Met

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Jab We Met" is a Bollywood romantic comedy about Aditya Kashyap, a heartbroken businessman who meets Geet, a talkative girl, on a train journey. They embark on a journey to her hometown in Punjab where they encounter challenges and form a deep bond. Aditya realizes he loves Geet but is hesitant to express his feelings. The movie is a heartwarming tale of love, friendship, and self-discovery with catchy songs and beautiful locations.

Phir Hera Pheri

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Phir Hera Pheri" is a Bollywood comedy sequel about Raju, Shyam, and Baburao who invest in a scheme to get rich quick, but end up in debt. They embark on a series of chaotic attempts to repay the debt, involving a kidnapping and run-in with a gangster. The movie is a laugh riot with witty one-liners and a talented cast, perfect for anyone who enjoys comedy.
